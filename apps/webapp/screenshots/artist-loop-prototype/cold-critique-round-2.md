## Discrimination result

Round 2 again found the palette-ramp and missing-contour plants, but it passed the off-world vocabulary plant and kept both intended controls below the pass bar. Across two blind critics, the visual critic was directionally useful on formal image properties and unreliable on semantic world-language compliance. The proposed kit therefore cannot treat one unconstrained cold-critic verdict as a gate: it needs an explicit per-axis rubric and human review, with a second independent critic or deterministic checks for any axis promoted to automation.
